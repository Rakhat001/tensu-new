# Screenshots

Place your app screenshots here. The following files are expected:

## Students App Screenshots
- `students-main.png` - Main screen with schedule
- `students-clubs.png` - Clubs list
- `students-memberships.png` - Memberships screen
- `students-payment.png` - Payment screen

## Staff App Screenshots
- `staff-main.png` - Main dashboard
- `staff-students.png` - Students management
- `staff-schedule.png` - Schedule management
- `staff-pricing.png` - Pricing management

## Usage
Screenshots should be:
- PNG format (recommended) or JPG
- Approximately 1080x2340 pixels (phone screen ratio)
- Clean app screenshots without device frames (the PhoneMockup component adds the frame)
